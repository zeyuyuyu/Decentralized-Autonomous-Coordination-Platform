# MoodMix - Your Daily Emotional Music Journey

## Overview
MoodMix is an open-source project that provides a personalized music experience based on your current emotional state. By analyzing your mood and preferences, the system recommends a curated playlist to enhance your daily life.

## Key Features
- **Mood Detection**: MoodMix uses advanced machine learning algorithms to detect your current emotional state in real-time, leveraging data from various sources (e.g., user input, facial recognition, biometrics).
- **Personalized Recommendations**: Based on your detected mood and listening history, the system generates a tailored playlist of songs and audio tracks to uplift, calm, or energize you.
- **Mood-Based Playlists**: MoodMix offers pre-curated playlists for common emotional states, such as 'Uplifting', 'Relaxing', 'Energetic', and 'Contemplative', allowing you to quickly access music suited to your current mood.
- **Emotion Tracking**: The app provides a history of your daily emotional states and the corresponding music recommendations, enabling you to better understand your mood patterns and the impact of music on your well-being.
- **Collaborative Filtering**: The system leverages the collective listening data of users to provide even more accurate and personalized music recommendations, taking advantage of the wisdom of the community.

## Getting Started
1. Install the MoodMix application on your device.
2. Grant the necessary permissions for mood detection and music playback.
3. Start using the app and let MoodMix guide you through your daily emotional music journey!
